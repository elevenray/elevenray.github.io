Intro

I am a soon-to-be Computer Science graduate at California State University, Fullerton. I am currently working towards a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science and a minor in mathematics.

I currently working at the university with Professor Paul Inventado in developing a programming auto-grading system. I have a strong interest in making educational tools and and helping other prospective computer science students learn more about the subject.

I am also interested in the subjects of Machine Learning and Data Science and currently am the President of the Data Science and Machine Learning Club at CSUF

Work

Software Developer Intern

I am an intern at Optimus Learning School in Brea, CA. Duties include: Focusing on HTML and PHP front-end system changes, test builds with TestFlight, work in a team to develop new web interfaces

Tutor and Teacher

I am a tutor and teacher at Optimus Learning School in Brea, CA . Duties include: Teaching python, Debugging student code in a quick manner, explaining Computer Science Topics, helping students set up environments (IDEs, virtualization, etc.) and generally helping students succeed.

More About Me

Outside of my career and education. I like to spend my free time making and collecting Keyboards. In a career with a lot of typing having a nice equipment is a luxury. Making Keyboards also helps me delve into the realm of amateur electronics assembly. As many custom keyboards run off of custom firmware developed using QMK.

Additionally, I also like to stay detail-oriented by making miniature model kits. I like to try to take them to the next level by painting and customizing them so they stay one of a kind. QMK.
